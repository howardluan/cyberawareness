Now, how to prevent this type of attack?

Even the URL (The link) looks real, it can be a phishing page without you know it.
To make sure it is not phishing page, one way is to click on the square to maximize the window.
If the link shows on the very top of your screen, you are much likely safe. If not, do NOT type anything and close the window immediately.
